<p><ul><li>Welded Tubes</li><li>Seamless Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon steel tubes are classified into two main categories: welded tubes and seamless tubes. Welded tubes are manufactured by welding together steel strips or plates, while seamless tubes are produced by piercing a solid billet of steel to form a tube without any seams. Welded tubes are typically less expensive and more readily available, while seamless tubes are known for their higher strength and more uniform composition. Each type of tube is used in various industries for applications such as construction, automotive, and industrial manufacturing.</p></p>
