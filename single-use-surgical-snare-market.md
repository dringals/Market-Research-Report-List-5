<p><p>A Single-Use Surgical Snare is a medical device used in endoscopic procedures to remove abnormal tissues or foreign objects from the body. It consists of a wire loop at the end of a long, flexible tube that is inserted into the body through a scope. Once the desired tissue or object is grasped, the loop is closed around it and the snare is tightened to remove it from the body.</p><p>The Single-Use Surgical Snare Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by factors such as an increasing number of endoscopic procedures being performed worldwide, rising prevalence of gastrointestinal disorders, and advancements in endoscopic technologies.
