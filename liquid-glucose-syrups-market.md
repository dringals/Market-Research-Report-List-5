<p><strong>The Liquid Glucose Syrups Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Conversion 20-35 DE</li><li>Intermediate Conversion 35-55 DE</li><li>High Conversion 55-70 DE</li><li>Very High Conversion 70-98 DE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid glucose syrups market is categorized based on dextrose equivalents (DE), which indicate the syrup's conversion level. Low conversion syrups (20-35 DE) are less sweet and used in specialty foods. Intermediate conversion syrups (35-55 DE) offer a balance of sweetness and viscosity, suitable for baked goods and beverages. High conversion syrups (55-70 DE) provide enhanced sweetness and moisture retention, widely used in confectionery. Very high conversion syrups (70-98 DE) are extremely sweet and appropriate for applications requiring high sugar content, such as coatings and sauces.</p></p>
