<p><p>Angular contact ball bearings are classified based on the contact angle between the balls and the inner and outer raceways. The market types include 15°, 25°, 30°, and 40°, referring to the angle at which the balls make contact. A smaller contact angle like 15° provides high-speed performance, while larger angles like 40° offer greater axial load capacity. Additionally, there are other market types with varying contact angles to cater to specific application requirements in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and machinery.</p></p>
