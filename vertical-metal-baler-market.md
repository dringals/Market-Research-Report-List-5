<p><p>Vertical metal balers are available in two main types: manual and PLC automatic. Manual balers require human intervention to operate, where the user manually controls the baling process. On the other hand, PLC automatic balers are equipped with programmable logic controllers that automate the baling process. These balers have sensors and controls that can regulate the compression force and bale ejection without the need for constant human supervision. Both types cater to different market segments based on the level of automation and control required.</p></p>
