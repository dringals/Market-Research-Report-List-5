<p><p>Rock climbing helmets can be categorized into three main types in the market: bubble, shell plastic, and other. Bubble helmets are characterized by a larger, rounder shape that provides overall protection for the head. Shell plastic helmets have a hard outer shell made of durable plastic that offers excellent impact resistance. The "other" category includes helmets made from different materials such as carbon fiber or hybrid designs with a mix of features from bubble and shell plastic helmets. Each type caters to different preferences and needs in the rock climbing community.</p></p>
