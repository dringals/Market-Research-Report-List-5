<p><p>RF SOI switches utilize silicon-on-insulator technology to achieve high performance in RF signal processing, offering low insertion loss and high isolation. They are commonly used in applications requiring high linearity and low power consumption, such as wireless communications and radar systems.</p><p>On the other hand, RF SOS switches utilize silicon-on-sapphire technology, which provides even lower parasitic capacitance and higher isolation compared to RF SOI switches. They are ideal for applications demanding ultra-low insertion loss and high power handling capacity, such as satellite communications and medical imaging systems.</p></p>
