<p><p>Disinfectant cleaners are categorized into different types based on the active ingredients they contain, which determine their effectiveness in killing germs. Chlorine disinfectants are commonly used for their strong antimicrobial properties. Peroxide disinfectants are known for their eco-friendly nature and ability to break down into water and oxygen. Aldehyde disinfectants are highly effective against a wide range of pathogens. Alcohol disinfectants are fast-acting and widely used in hospitals. Iodine-containing disinfectants are effective against bacteria, viruses, and fungi. Phenol disinfectants are commonly used in household cleaning products. Other types of disinfectant cleaners include quaternary ammonium compounds and hydrogen peroxide-based cleaners.</p></p>
