<p><p>Circulator pumps are divided into two main categories: vertical circulator pumps and horizontal circulator pumps. Vertical circulator pumps are designed to be installed vertically, typically used in applications where space is limited. Both types of circulator pumps are commonly used in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems to circulate hot or cold water throughout a building.</p></p>
