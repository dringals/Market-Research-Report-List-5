<p><p>Electrodialysis equipment market consists of two main types: Continuous Electrodialysis and Batch Electrodialysis. Continuous Electrodialysis involves the continuous flow of feed solution and production of purified and concentrated products simultaneously. This type is efficient for large-scale operations. On the other hand, Batch Electrodialysis operates by processing feed solutions in batches, making it suitable for smaller scale operations or when specific processing conditions are required. Both types play a crucial role in various industries for separation and purification processes.</p></p>
