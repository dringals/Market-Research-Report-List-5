<p><strong>The Chemical Diaphragm Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Mechanical</li><li>Solenoid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical diaphragm pumps are divided into three main types: hydraulic, mechanical, and solenoid. Hydraulic diaphragm pumps use water or another liquid as a power source to move the diaphragm and pump chemicals. Mechanical diaphragm pumps use a mechanical linkage or connection to move the diaphragm and pump fluids. Solenoid diaphragm pumps use an electromagnetic solenoid to control the movement of the diaphragm and pump liquids. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the chemical industry.</p></p>
