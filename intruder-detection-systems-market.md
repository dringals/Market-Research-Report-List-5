<p><p>Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS) market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Network Intrusion Detection Systems (NIDS)** monitor network traffic for suspicious activities across devices to identify potential threats. </p><p>2. **Host-based Intrusion Detection Systems (HIDS)** operate on individual devices, analyzing system logs and file integrity to detect intrusions. </p><p>3. **Perimeter Intrusion Detection Systems (PIDS)** are deployed at physical or digital boundaries to monitor unauthorized access. </p><p>4. **VM-based Intrusion Detection Systems (VMIDS)** focus on virtualized environments, ensuring security in virtual machines by detecting malicious activities specific to their architecture.</p></p>
