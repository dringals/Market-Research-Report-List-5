<p><p>Whole house water filters can be categorized into two types - with ultrafiltration membrane and without ultrafiltration membrane. Filters with ultrafiltration membrane utilize a highly efficient membrane to remove even the smallest contaminants from the water, providing clean and safe water for the entire household. On the other hand, filters without ultrafiltration membrane rely on other filtration methods such as carbon filters to remove impurities. Both types of filters offer different levels of filtration and cater to different household water purification needs.</p></p>
