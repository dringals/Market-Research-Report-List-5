<p><p>Rotary Vane Vacuum Pumps are categorized into two main types: Single Stage and Two Stage. Single Stage Rotary Vane Vacuum Pumps are designed to efficiently create a vacuum by using a single rotor to compress air in a single stage. On the other hand, Two Stage Rotary Vane Vacuum Pumps utilize two rotors to compress air in two separate stages, resulting in a higher ultimate vacuum level. Both types of pumps are widely used in various industries for different applications based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
