<p><p>Nerve repair market types include nerve wraps, nerve protectors, nerve connectors, and nerve grafts. </p><p>Nerve wraps are used to provide a protective barrier around damaged nerves to promote healing. Nerve protectors are devices that shield nerves from external damage during surgery or injury. Nerve connectors are tools that help bridge the gap between severed nerve ends, facilitating regeneration. Nerve grafts involve transferring healthy nerves from one part of the body to another to restore function in damaged nerves. These products play critical roles in aiding nerve repair and recovery.</p></p>
