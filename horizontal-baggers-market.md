<p><p>Horizontal baggers in the packaging industry can be classified into two main market types based on their motion capabilities: Continuous Motion and Intermittent Motion. Continuous Motion baggers are designed to operate at a constant speed, allowing for high-speed and continuous packaging of products. On the other hand, Intermittent Motion baggers have a start-stop motion, making them suitable for packaging products that require precise placement or handling. Both market types cater to different packaging requirements and offer versatility in the packaging process.</p></p>
