<p><p>Sifting machines are classified into two main types based on the material of the mesh used for sieving: metal mesh sifting machines and plastic mesh sifting machines. Metal mesh sifting machines are commonly used for heavy-duty industrial applications due to their durability and resistance to high temperatures. On the other hand, plastic mesh sifting machines are preferred for lighter applications and food processing industries as they are easy to clean, corrosion-resistant, and cost-effective. Both types of sifting machines play a crucial role in various industries for efficient material separation.</p></p>
