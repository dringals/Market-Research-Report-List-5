<p><p>Pipe cleaning robots are categorized into two main types: pressure-based cleaning robots and tool-based cleaning robots. Pressure-based cleaning robots utilize high-pressure water jets to clean pipes effectively, while tool-based cleaning robots use various mechanical tools and brushes to remove debris and blockages from pipes. Both types of robots offer efficient and precise cleaning solutions for a wide range of pipe diameters and materials, making them ideal choices for industries such as construction, wastewater management, and manufacturing.</p></p>
