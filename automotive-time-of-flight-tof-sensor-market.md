<p><ul><li>Direct ToF Sensors</li><li>Indirect ToF Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Time of Flight (ToF) sensors can be categorized into two main types: Direct ToF sensors and Indirect ToF sensors. Direct ToF sensors measure the time taken for light to travel directly from the sensor to the object and back, providing highly accurate distance measurements. In contrast, Indirect ToF sensors use other methods such as triangulation or structured light to measure distance. Both types of ToF sensors have their own advantages and applications in the automotive industry for applications such as collision avoidance and autonomous driving.</p></p>
