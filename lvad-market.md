<p><p>LVAD (Left Ventricular Assist Device) Market Types include Bridge-to-Transplant Therapy (BTT), Bridge-to-Destination Therapy (BTD), Destination Therapy (DT), and Bridge-to-Recovery Therapy (BTR). BTT therapy is used as a temporary solution while a patient waits for a heart transplant. BTD therapy is intended for patients who may be eligible for a transplant in the future. DT therapy is for patients who are not transplant candidates and need long-term support. BTR therapy is used for patients who may recover normal heart function with temporary LVAD support.</p></p>
