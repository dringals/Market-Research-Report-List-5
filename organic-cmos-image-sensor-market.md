<p><p>The organic CMOS image sensor market is primarily divided into two types: linear image sensors and area image sensors. Linear image sensors capture images one line at a time, making them ideal for applications like barcode readers and scanners. In contrast, area image sensors capture entire images at once, suitable for applications such as digital cameras and smartphones. The growing demand for high-quality imaging and energy-efficient devices drives the development and adoption of both sensor types in various industries.</p></p>
