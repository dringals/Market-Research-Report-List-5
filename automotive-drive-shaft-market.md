<p><p>The automotive drive shaft market consists of two main types: half shafts and propeller shafts. Half shafts are commonly used in front-wheel-drive vehicles and are responsible for transmitting power from the transmission to the wheels. Propeller shafts, on the other hand, are used in rear-wheel-drive vehicles and transfer power from the transmission to the rear differential. Both types of drive shafts play a crucial role in ensuring smooth power delivery and efficient performance in a vehicle.</p></p>
