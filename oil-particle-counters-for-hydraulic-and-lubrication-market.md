There are three main types of oil particle counters in the market: handheld particle counters, desktop particle counters, and in-line particle counters. Handheld particle counters are portable and can be used for on-site testing. Desktop particle counters are stationary and are typically used in labs. In-line particle counters are integrated directly into the hydraulic or lubrication system for continuous monitoring.</p></p>
