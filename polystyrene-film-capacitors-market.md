<p><p>The Polystyrene Film Capacitors market comprises mainly two types: Axial and Radial. </p><p>Axial Polystyrene Film Capacitors feature leads extending from either end, allowing for linear placement on circuit boards; they are often used in applications requiring stability and precision. </p><p>Radial Polystyrene Film Capacitors have leads positioned on the same axis, facilitating compact designs and easier mounting in tighter spaces. Both types are favored for their low dielectric losses and temperature stability, making them ideal for high-frequency and timing applications.</p></p>
