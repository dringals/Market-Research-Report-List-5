<p><p>Natural convection incubators are laboratory devices designed to provide a stable environment for the growth of microorganisms, cell cultures, and embryos. These incubators rely on the natural circulation of air to evenly distribute heat without the use of mechanical fans, ensuring gentle and consistent temperature conditions. This makes them ideal for sensitive biological processes where vibration or turbulence from fans might adversely affect the samples.</p><p>The Natural Convection Incubator Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.10% during the forecast period.
