<p><p>Flexion Tires are categorized into Very High Flexion (VF) and High Flexion (HF) based on their ability to flex under heavy loads. Very High Flexion tires have the highest flexibility and can carry heavy loads at low inflation pressures, providing better traction and reduced soil compaction. High Flexion tires have a slightly lower level of flexibility compared to VF tires but still offer improved performance under heavy loads. Both types of tires are designed to enhance productivity and efficiency in agricultural and industrial applications.</p></p>
