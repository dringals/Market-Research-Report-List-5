<p><p>The CCTV telephoto zoom lens market primarily includes two types: C-Mount and CS-Mount lenses. C-Mount lenses have a 1-inch thread and typically offer greater versatility for longer focal lengths, making them suitable for various surveillance applications. In contrast, CS-Mount lenses are designed with a shorter flange distance, allowing them to be used more easily with compact camera systems. Both lens types enable adjustable zoom capabilities, enhancing detail capture in diverse CCTV setups while addressing the specific needs of surveillance environments.</p></p>
