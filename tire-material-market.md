<p><p>The tire material market encompasses various components essential for tire manufacturing. Elastomers serve as the primary rubber base, offering flexibility and durability. Reinforcing fillers, like carbon black and silica, enhance strength and wear resistance. Plasticizers improve processability and flexibility. Chemicals, including curing agents and antioxidants, contribute to performance and longevity. Metal reinforcements, such as steel belts, add structural integrity, while textile reinforcements, often made from nylon or polyester, provide additional strength and support, ensuring optimal tire performance.</p></p>
