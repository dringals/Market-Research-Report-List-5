<p><p>Veterinary treatment ozone therapy units are used in various applications such as hyperbaric major autohemotherapy, normobaric minor autohemotherapy, injection and syringe therapy, insufflation, water ozonisation, and others. These units are designed to deliver ozone therapy to animals for treating a wide range of health issues. Hyperbaric autohemotherapy involves exposing the blood to ozone under pressure, while normobaric autohemotherapy is done at normal atmospheric pressure. Injection and syringe therapy involves directly injecting ozone into the body, while insufflation is the process of administering ozone rectally, vaginally, or nasally. Water ozonisation refers to the treatment of water with ozone before it is given to animals.</p></p>
