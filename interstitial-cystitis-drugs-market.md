<p><p>Interstitial Cystitis Drugs market can be categorized into two main types: Oral Therapy and Intravesical Therapy. Oral therapy involves the administration of drugs via the mouth, which are then absorbed into the bloodstream to reach the bladder. On the other hand, Intravesical Therapy involves the direct instillation of medications into the bladder through a catheter. Both types of therapy aim to reduce inflammation, pain, and frequency of urination associated with interstitial cystitis, providing relief to patients suffering from this condition.</p></p>
