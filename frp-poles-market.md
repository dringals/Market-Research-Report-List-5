<p><strong>The FRP Poles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Filament Winding</li><li>Pultrusion</li><li>Centrifugal Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FRP poles market types include filament winding, pultrusion, and centrifugal casting. Filament winding involves wrapping continuous fibers around a mandrel and impregnating them with resin to create strong and lightweight poles. Pultrusion involves pulling fibers through a resin bath and then through a heated die to form the shape of the pole. Centrifugal casting involves pouring resin and fibers into a rotating mold to create poles with a hollow core. Each method offers unique benefits in terms of strength, durability, and cost-effectiveness in the FRP poles market.</p></p>
