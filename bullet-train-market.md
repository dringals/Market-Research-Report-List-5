<p><p>The bullet train market consists of two main types: wheel on rail and Maglev. Wheel on rail technology utilizes traditional steel wheels and tracks to propel the train at high speeds, while Maglev trains use magnetic levitation to hover above the track, reducing friction and allowing for even faster speeds. Both technologies have their advantages and limitations, with wheel on rail being more cost-effective and widespread, while Maglev offers smoother rides and higher speeds but at a higher cost.</p></p>
