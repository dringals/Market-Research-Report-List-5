<p><ul><li>SMD Quartz Crystal Resonator</li><li>DIP Quartz Crystal Resonator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crystal resonators market is primarily divided into two types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) quartz crystal resonators and DIP (Dual In-line Package) quartz crystal resonators. SMD quartz crystal resonators are compact and designed for surface mounting on circuit boards, offering benefits like reduced space and enhanced performance for modern electronics. Conversely, DIP quartz crystal resonators feature a traditional pin configuration, favored in through-hole applications. Both types serve critical roles in timing and frequency stabilization across various electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
