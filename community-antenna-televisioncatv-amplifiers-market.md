<p><p>Community Antenna Television (CATV) amplifiers are essential components in the distribution of cable TV signals to households. There are two main types of CATV amplifiers - outdoor and indoor. Outdoor CATV amplifiers are typically installed on utility poles or in underground vaults to boost signal strength for long-distance transmission. Indoor CATV amplifiers are usually placed inside buildings or cabinets to enhance signal quality for shorter distances. Both types of amplifiers play a crucial role in ensuring clear and reliable TV reception for consumers.</p></p>
