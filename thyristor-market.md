<p><p>Thyristors are semiconductor devices widely used in power control applications. Unidirectional Thyristors allow current flow in only one direction, making them suitable for rectifier and power switching circuits. On the other hand, Bidirectional Thyristors can control current flow in both directions, making them ideal for applications requiring bidirectional power control like AC switches and motor drives. Both types of Thyristors play a crucial role in managing power distribution and control in various industries.</p></p>
