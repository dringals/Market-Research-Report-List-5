<p><p>Nickel Based Superalloys market can be categorized into different types based on their composition. The main types include Ni-Cu, Ni-Cr, Ni-Mo, and Others. Ni-Cu superalloys contain nickel and copper, offering high strength and corrosion resistance. Ni-Cr superalloys contain nickel and chromium, providing excellent high-temperature strength and oxidation resistance. Ni-Mo superalloys contain nickel and molybdenum, offering improved toughness and creep resistance. The "Others" category includes superalloys with different compositions tailored for specific applications in industries such as aerospace and power generation.</p></p>
