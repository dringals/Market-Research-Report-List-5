<p><strong>The Aviation Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Directional Antenna</li><li>Omnidirectional Antenna</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Directional antennas are designed to focus their signal in one specific direction, providing a high gain and longer range. These antennas are commonly used for long-distance communication, such as air-to-ground or air-to-air communication.</p><p>Omnidirectional antennas radiate their signal in all directions, providing a 360-degree coverage area. These antennas are commonly used for short-range communication within a confined area, such as within an aircraft or airport. They are ideal for applications where the signal needs to reach multiple points simultaneously.</p></p>
