<p><p>Parking aid sensors are designed to assist drivers in navigating tight spaces and avoiding obstacles while parking. There are two main types of parking aid sensors: front parking sensors and rear parking sensors. Front parking sensors are typically located on the front bumper of a vehicle and help drivers judge distance from objects in front of the vehicle. Rear parking sensors are located on the rear bumper and assist drivers in reversing by alerting them to obstacles behind the vehicle. Both types of sensors help improve safety and ease of parking for drivers.</p></p>
