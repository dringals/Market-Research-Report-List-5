<p><p>The Creatine Kinase Test Kit market consists of two main types: the Enzyme Coupling Method and the Colorimetry Method. </p><p>The Enzyme Coupling Method measures the activity of creatine kinase by detecting the production of adenosine triphosphate, which is directly proportional to the enzyme activity. </p><p>On the other hand, the Colorimetry Method measures the level of creatine kinase by utilizing a color reaction that changes in intensity based on the concentration of the enzyme in the sample. Both methods are widely used in clinical settings for diagnosing muscle and heart conditions.</p></p>
