<p><p>Nonwovens for Filtration market offers a variety of types including Airlaid, Wetlaid, Spunlaid, Meltblown, Needlepunching, Hydro-entanglement, and Thermobonded. Airlaid nonwovens are made using air to blend fibers, Wetlaid involves a wet web forming process, Spunlaid utilizes continuous filament fibers, Meltblown uses heated air to form fibers, Needlepunching involves mechanically interlocking fibers, Hydro-entanglement uses high-pressure water jets, and Thermobonded involves bonding fibers using heat and pressure. Each type offers unique properties and applications for filtration purposes.</p></p>
