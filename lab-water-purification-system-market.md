<p><p>Lab Water Purification System Market includes two main types: Point of Use Systems and Large Central Systems. Point of Use Systems are compact units installed directly at the point of water consumption, providing purified water on demand. They are ideal for laboratories with limited space. On the other hand, Large Central Systems are designed to support multiple users and are typically installed in a central location within a laboratory or research facility. They are more suitable for high-volume water purification needs. Both systems play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and purity of water used in lab experiments and processes.</p></p>
