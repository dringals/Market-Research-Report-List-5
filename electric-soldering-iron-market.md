<p><strong>The Electric Soldering Iron Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Heated Soldering Iron</li><li>Internal Heated Soldering Iron</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric soldering iron market is primarily divided into two types: external heated and internal heated soldering irons. External heated soldering irons feature a heating element located outside the soldering tip, allowing for quicker temperature adjustments and easier replacement of tips. In contrast, internal heated soldering irons have their heating elements housed within the tip, providing consistent heat but reducing the speed of tip changes. Both types cater to various applications, ranging from electronics assembly to hobbyist projects, each with unique advantages.</p></p>
