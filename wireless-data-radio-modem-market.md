<p><p>Wireless data radio modems are classified into two main types based on their range capabilities: short range and long range. Short range radio modems are designed to transmit data over relatively close distances, typically within a confined area or building. They are commonly used for applications like industrial automation and home networking. In contrast, long range radio modems are capable of sending data over much larger distances, making them suitable for applications like outdoor communication systems and remote monitoring.</p></p>
