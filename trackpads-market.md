Multi-touch trackpads can detect multiple points of contact simultaneously, enabling more complex gestures like pinch-to-zoom and two-finger scrolling. The market for single-touch trackpads is more basic and affordable, while multi-touch trackpads cater to users who require more advanced functionality and precision.</p></p>
