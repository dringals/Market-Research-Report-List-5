<p><p>Head-up Displays (HUD) project essential information onto a transparent surface, allowing users to view data without looking away from their primary focus. The C-HUD, or Conventional HUD, provides basic information like speed and navigation. W-HUD offers enhanced features, displaying more complex data while maintaining user safety. AR-HUD, or Augmented Reality HUD, integrates real-world visuals with digital overlays, offering an immersive experience. These types serve diverse applications, particularly in automotive and aviation industries, enhancing situational awareness and user experience.</p></p>
