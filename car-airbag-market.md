<p><p>The car airbag market consists of several types, each designed for specific safety needs. Frontal airbags deploy from the steering wheel and dashboard to protect occupants during head-on collisions. Side airbags inflate from the side panels to shield against side impacts. Curtain airbags drop down from the roof to protect passengers' heads in rollover scenarios. Knee airbags target knee and lower leg protection in frontal crashes. Other market segments may include specialized airbags for unique vehicle designs and purposes.</p></p>
