<p><p>Exterior wall systems market includes ventilated façade, curtain walls, and non-ventilated façade systems. Ventilated façade systems allow for air circulation between the exterior cladding and insulation, providing thermal efficiency. Curtain walls are typically non-load bearing and mostly made of glass, providing a transparent and aesthetically pleasing exterior. Non-ventilated facades are sealed systems that do not allow for air circulation, often used for better sound insulation and weather resistance. Each system offers unique benefits and applications in the construction industry.</p></p>
