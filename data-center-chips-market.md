Application Specific Integrated Circuit (ASIC) chips are customized for specific applications, providing high efficiency and performance. Field Programmable Gate Array (FPGA) chips offer flexibility and reconfigurability for various applications. Central Processing Unit (CPU) chips are the general-purpose processors used for overall computing tasks. Other types of chips in the market include specialized processors and accelerators for specific functions within data centers.</p></p>
