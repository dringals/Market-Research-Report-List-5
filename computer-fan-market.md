<p><p>The computer fan market can be divided into two main types: AC fans and DC fans. AC fans are powered by alternating current from a power source, making them suitable for larger and more powerful cooling applications. On the other hand, DC fans run on direct current and are often used in smaller electronic devices such as laptops and desktop computers. Both types of fans play a crucial role in maintaining the temperature of electronic devices and preventing overheating.</p></p>
