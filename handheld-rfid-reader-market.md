<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Handheld RFID Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency Handheld RFID readers</li><li>High Frequency Handheld RFID readers</li><li>Ultra High Frequency handheld RFID readers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld RFID reader market is categorized into three main types based on frequency: </p><p>1. **Low Frequency Handheld RFID Readers** operate typically at 125 kHz to 134 kHz, ideal for applications like animal tracking and access control due to their ability to read through various materials. </p><p>2. **High Frequency Handheld RFID Readers** function at 13.56 MHz, commonly used for library management and ticketing systems, offering faster data transfer rates and shorter reading ranges. </p><p>3. **Ultra High Frequency Handheld RFID Readers** work between 860 MHz and 960 MHz, suitable for logistics and inventory management, providing long-range capabilities and faster data acquisition.</p></p>
