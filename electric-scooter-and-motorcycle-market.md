<p><p>The electric scooter and motorcycle market can be categorized based on battery types: Sealed Lead Acid (SLA) and Lithium-Ion (Li-Ion). SLA batteries are cost-effective and robust but heavier, offering shorter range and longer charging times. In contrast, Li-Ion batteries provide higher energy density, lighter weight, and faster charging, resulting in a longer range and improved performance. As demand for eco-friendly transportation grows, Li-Ion batteries are increasingly favored for their efficiency, contributing to a shift towards advanced electric mobility solutions.</p></p>
