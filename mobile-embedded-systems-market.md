<p><p>Mobile Embedded Systems Market includes two main types: Embedded Hardware Market and Embedded Software Market. </p><p>Embedded Hardware Market focuses on the physical components of mobile devices, such as processors, sensors, and connectivity modules. It includes products like microcontrollers, application-specific integrated circuits (ASICs), and field-programmable gate arrays (FPGAs).</p><p>Embedded Software Market, on the other hand, deals with the software that is built into these hardware components to control their functions. This includes operating systems, device drivers, and application software specifically designed for embedded systems.</p></p>
