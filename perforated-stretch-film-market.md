<p><p>Perforated stretch film comes in two main types: Perforated Manual Film and Perforated Machine Film. </p><p>Perforated Manual Film is designed for manual application where the user tears off sections of film as needed. This type is commonly used for wrapping smaller or irregularly shaped items.</p><p>Perforated Machine Film is designed for use with automated wrapping machines. The perforations allow for consistent tearing and application, making it ideal for high-volume operations. Both types offer the benefit of reducing waste and increasing efficiency in packaging operations.</p></p>
