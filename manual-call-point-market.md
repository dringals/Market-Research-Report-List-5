<p><p>Manual call points are crucial for fire safety as they enable individuals to manually activate a fire alarm in case of emergency. These call points are available in two main market types: wired and wireless. Wired manual call points are connected to the fire alarm system through physical cables, offering a reliable and stable connection. On the other hand, wireless call points are wire-free and communicate with the fire alarm system using radio signals, providing flexibility and ease of installation in various settings. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
