<p><p>Carbon steel forgings are classified into various types based on their shapes and uses. Seamless rolled rings are circular components used in heavy machinery and equipment. Flanges are discs with protruding rims used to connect pipes or valves. Blocks are solid rectangular pieces used in structural applications. Shafts and stepped shafts are cylindrical components used for transmitting power. Discs and hubs are flat circular components used in machinery. Cylinders and sleeves are cylindrical components used in hydraulic systems and machinery.</p></p>
