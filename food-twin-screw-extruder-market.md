<p><strong>The Food Twin-screw Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Co-rotating</li><li>Parallel Counter-rotating</li><li>Conical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food twin-screw extruders come in three main types - parallel co-rotating, parallel counter-rotating, and conical. Parallel co-rotating extruders have screws that rotate in the same direction, producing high-output and high-torque applications. Parallel counter-rotating extruders have screws that rotate in opposite directions, providing better mixing and self-cleaning capabilities. Conical extruders have tapered screws which help in conveying and compressing materials efficiently. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications in the food industry.</p></p>
