<p><p>Hot bonders are used in aerospace and automotive industries for composite repair applications. There are two market types based on the number of heating zones: Single zone and Dual zone. Single zone hot bonders have one heating zone and are suitable for simple repairs. Dual zone hot bonders have two independent heating zones, allowing for more complex repairs on larger components or multiple repairs simultaneously. Both types are crucial for maintaining the structural integrity of composite materials and ensuring safe and effective repairs.</p></p>
