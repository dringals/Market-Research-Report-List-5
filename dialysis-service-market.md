<p><strong>The Dialysis Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hemodialysis</li><li>Peritoneal Dialysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dialysis services market comprises two main types: hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis. Hemodialysis involves the use of a machine to filter blood, usually done in a hospital or dialysis center. On the other hand, peritoneal dialysis involves the use of the patient's peritoneum as a filter, allowing for more flexibility as it can be done at home. Both types of dialysis have their own market segments and cater to individuals with different needs and preferences in managing kidney failure.</p></p>
