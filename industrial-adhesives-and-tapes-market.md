<p><p>Industrial adhesives and tapes market types include hot melt, contact spray, acrylic, anaerobic adhesive, epoxy adhesives, and other specialized adhesives. Hot melt adhesives are solid at room temperature and melt when heated, while contact spray adhesives are sprayed on surfaces to create a strong bond. Acrylic adhesives are known for their durability and resistance to environmental factors. Anaerobic adhesives cure in the absence of air, while epoxy adhesives are versatile and strong. Other specialized adhesives cater to specific industry needs such as high temperature resistance or electrical conductivity.</p></p>
