<p><strong>The Glass FIber Nonwoven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet-Laid Process</li><li>Dry-Laid Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glass Fiber Nonwoven Market comprises two primary types: the Wet-Laid Process and the Dry-Laid Process. The Wet-Laid Process involves mixing glass fibers with water and additives, creating a fiber slurry that is subsequently dried on a mesh to form a nonwoven fabric. Conversely, the Dry-Laid Process utilizes mechanical means to spread dry glass fibers onto a conveyor, subsequently bonding them with heat or adhesive. Both methods are essential for producing diverse nonwoven applications in industries such as automotive, construction, and filtration.</p></p>
