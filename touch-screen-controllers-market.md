<p><p>Resistive Touch Controllers rely on pressure to detect touch input, making them suitable for precision applications. They are commonly used in devices like ATMs and industrial equipment. Capacitive Touch Controllers, on the other hand, detect touch through the electrical properties of the human body. This allows for multi-touch input and better durability, making them popular in smartphones and tablets. Both types of controllers play a crucial role in the touch screen market, catering to different needs and preferences.</p></p>
