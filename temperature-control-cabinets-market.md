<p><p>Temperature control cabinets come in two main types, manual and automatic. Manual cabinets require the user to manually adjust settings such as temperature and humidity. These cabinets are generally more affordable but require more frequent monitoring and adjustments. On the other hand, automatic cabinets have built-in sensors and controls that adjust settings as needed, making them more convenient but also more expensive. Both types of cabinets are essential for maintaining the proper temperature and humidity levels for various applications, such as storing pharmaceuticals or food items.</p></p>
