<p><ul><li>Standard Duty</li><li>Washdown Duty</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mounted ball bearings are commonly classified into two main types: Standard Duty and Washdown Duty. Standard Duty mounted ball bearings are designed for general purpose industrial applications where moderate loads and speeds are expected. On the other hand, Washdown Duty mounted ball bearings are specifically designed for use in harsh environments that require frequent washdowns, such as food processing plants or outdoor applications where exposure to water or chemicals is a concern. These bearings are constructed with corrosion-resistant materials and sealed to prevent ingress of contaminants.</p></p>
