<p><p>The Automotive Thick Film Resistors market can be divided into two main types: SMD Type and Through Hole Type. SMD (Surface Mount Device) Type resistors are mounted directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board, making them compact and suitable for high-density applications. On the other hand, Through Hole Type resistors are mounted through holes in the circuit board, providing greater stability and heat dissipation. Both types cater to different requirements in the automotive industry, offering reliability and performance in various applications.</p></p>
