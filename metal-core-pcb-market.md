<p><p>The Metal Core PCB market is categorized into three primary types: Aluminum Core PCBs, Copper Core PCBs, and Alloys Core PCBs. Aluminum Core PCBs are widely used for their excellent thermal conductivity, lightweight properties, and cost-effectiveness, making them ideal for LED applications. Copper Core PCBs offer superior thermal dissipation and electrical conductivity, suitable for high-performance applications. Alloys Core PCBs utilize a combination of materials to enhance thermal management and mechanical integrity, catering to specialized demands in advanced electronics and automotive sectors.</p></p>
