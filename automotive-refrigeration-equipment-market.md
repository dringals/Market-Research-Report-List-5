<p><ul><li>Single Temperature Type</li><li>Multi-Temperature Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive refrigeration equipment market is categorized into two main types: Single Temperature Type and Multi-Temperature Type. Single Temperature Type refrigeration systems are designed to maintain a consistent temperature throughout the entire refrigerated space, whereas Multi-Temperature Type systems allow for different compartments within the same refrigerated space to be maintained at different temperatures simultaneously. Single Temperature Type is more commonly used in vehicles transporting a single type of cargo, while Multi-Temperature Type is utilized in vehicles carrying a variety of temperature-sensitive goods.</p></p>
