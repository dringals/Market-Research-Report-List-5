<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Industrial Dental 3D Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stereolithography (SLA)</li><li>Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM)</li><li>Digital Light Processing (DLP)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial dental 3D printers are categorized into different types based on their technology. Stereolithography (SLA) printers use a UV laser to cure liquid resin into solid layers. Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) printers extrude heated thermoplastic filament to build the model layer by layer. Digital Light Processing (DLP) printers use a digital light projector to cure liquid resin into solid layers. Other types of industrial dental 3D printers include Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) and Continuous Digital Light Manufacturing (CDLM) printers, each utilizing different methods to create accurate dental models.</p></p>
