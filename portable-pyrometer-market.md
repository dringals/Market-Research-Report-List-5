<p><p>Portable pyrometers are used for non-contact temperature measurement in various industries. There are two main types of portable pyrometers on the market: optical technology and infrared technology. Optical technology pyrometers use the principle of measuring the emitted radiation from a target to determine its temperature, while infrared technology pyrometers use infrared radiation to calculate temperature without physical contact. Both types of portable pyrometers offer accurate and reliable temperature measurements, making them essential tools in industrial processes where precise temperature control is crucial.</p></p>
