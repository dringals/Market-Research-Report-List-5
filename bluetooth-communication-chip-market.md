<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Dual Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bluetooth communication chips are available in two main types: single mode and dual mode. Single mode chips are designed for low-power, low-cost devices that only need to communicate with other Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) devices. Dual mode chips, on the other hand, are suitable for devices that need to communicate with both BLE and traditional Bluetooth devices. The choice between single mode and dual mode chips depends on the specific requirements of the device and the desired functionality.</p></p>
