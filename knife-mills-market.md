<p><ul><li>Pulley Knife Mills</li><li>Without Pulley Knife Mills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Knife mills are commonly classified into two main types based on the presence of a pulley system: pulley knife mills and without pulley knife mills. Pulley knife mills are equipped with a pulley system that allows for easy adjustment and control of the milling process. On the other hand, without pulley knife mills do not have a pulley system, making them typically more compact and simpler in design. Both types of knife mills are used in various industries for size reduction and particle homogenization applications.</p></p>
