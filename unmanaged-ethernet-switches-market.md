<p><p>Unmanaged Ethernet switches are networking devices that connect multiple devices within a local area network (LAN) without the need for configuration. They operate at Layer 2 of the OSI model and are primarily used for straightforward plug-and-play applications, allowing devices to communicate with one another smoothly. These switches simplify network deployment due to their ease of use, making them ideal for small businesses, home networks, and environments where minimal setup is required.</p><p>The Unmanaged Ethernet Switches Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.80% during the forecast period.
