<p><p>5G network infrastructure market includes different types of cell networks. Femto cell is a small cellular base station meant for use in residential or small business environments. Pico cell covers a slightly larger area than femto cells, typically used in indoor environments. Micro cell is larger and covers a wider area like a neighborhood or a shopping mall. Macro cell is the largest and most common cell type, covering large geographic areas like cities or rural regions. Each type serves different purposes in providing efficient and reliable 5G network coverage.</p></p>
