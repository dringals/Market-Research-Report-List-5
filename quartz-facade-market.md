<p><p>Press molding and casting molding are two types of methods used in the quartz facade market. Press molding involves compressing quartz particles into a mold under high pressure to create the desired shape, while casting molding involves pouring liquid quartz into a mold and allowing it to harden into the desired form. Both methods are popular in the market for their ability to create intricate designs and precise shapes, catering to different preferences and requirements of customers.</p></p>
