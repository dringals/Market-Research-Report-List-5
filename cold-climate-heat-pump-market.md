<p><p>Cold-climate heat pumps are gaining popularity in regions with harsh winters, offering efficient heating solutions. Two main types of cold-climate heat pump markets are ductless heat pumps and central-ducted heat pumps. Ductless heat pumps are ideal for single-room or zone heating, providing flexibility and energy savings. Central-ducted heat pumps are suitable for whole-house heating, distributing warm air through ductwork. Both types offer cost-effective and environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional heating systems in cold climates.</p></p>
