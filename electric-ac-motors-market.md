<p><ul><li>Synchronous</li><li>Induction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric AC Motors come in two main types - Synchronous and Induction motors. Synchronous motors operate at a fixed speed and are ideal for applications requiring precise speed control, such as in industrial machinery. Induction motors, on the other hand, are more commonly used due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. They work by inducing a current in the rotor, causing it to rotate. Induction motors are widely used in household appliances, HVAC systems, and commercial equipment due to their reliability and versatility.</p></p>
