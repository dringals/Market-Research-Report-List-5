<p><ul><li>Round Type</li><li>Square Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RGB filters are commonly available in two market types: round type and square type. Round type filters are typically preferred for cameras with circular lens mounts, as they align perfectly with the lens. On the other hand, square type filters are designed for cameras with square or rectangular lens mounts. Both types of filters are widely used in photography and videography to enhance color accuracy and control light entering the lens. Customers can choose the type of filter based on their specific camera model and requirements.</p></p>
