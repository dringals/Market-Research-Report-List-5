<p><p>Oil separators are used to remove oil from water or air streams in various industrial applications. Different types of oil separators available in the market include filter type oil separators, centrifugal oil separators, and other specialized separators. Filter type oil separators use filters to trap oil droplets from the stream, while centrifugal oil separators rely on centrifugal force to separate oil from water or air.
