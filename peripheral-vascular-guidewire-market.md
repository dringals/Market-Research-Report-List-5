<p><p>A Peripheral Vascular Guidewire is a medical device that is used to navigate the peripheral vascular system during various procedures such as angioplasty, stenting, and atherectomy. These guidewires are designed to be flexible and maneuverable, allowing for easy navigation through the arteries and veins. They are crucial tools in minimally invasive procedures for the treatment of peripheral vascular diseases.</p><p>The Peripheral Vascular Guidewire Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.60% during the forecast period. The growing prevalence of peripheral vascular diseases, technological advancements in guidewire design, and increasing preference for minimally invasive procedures are some of the key factors driving the market growth.
