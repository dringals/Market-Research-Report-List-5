On the other hand, Multi Particle Cyclotron accelerates multiple particles simultaneously, allowing for a wider range of beam options and applications in medical imaging and radiotherapy. Both types play a crucial role in advancing medical technology and improving patient care through precise and efficient treatment options.</p></p>
