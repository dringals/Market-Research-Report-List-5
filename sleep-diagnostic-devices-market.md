<p><p>The Sleep Diagnostic Devices Market consists of four main types: Full Attended Polysomnography (PSG), Portable Unattended PSG, Four-Channel Home Sleep Test (HST), and Three-Channel HST. Full Attended PSG involves monitoring various physiological parameters during sleep in a sleep lab. Portable Unattended PSG allows patients to sleep in their own beds while being monitored. Four-Channel HST measures four physiological parameters at home, while Three-Channel HST measures three parameters. These devices are used to diagnose sleep disorders such as sleep apnea and insomnia.</p></p>
