<p><p>The ZnSe window market is categorized into three main types: uncoated, anti-reflective (AR) coated, and other coating options. Uncoated ZnSe windows offer basic protection and are cost-effective, but may exhibit higher reflectance. AR-coated windows are designed to minimize reflections, enhancing transmission efficiency for applications like lasers. Other coating options may include coatings for specific wavelengths or environmental resistance, catering to diverse needs in sectors such as optics, telecommunications, and infrared systems, thereby enhancing performance in various applications.</p></p>
