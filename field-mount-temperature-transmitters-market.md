<p><p>Field mount temperature transmitters are used in various industries to measure temperature and transmit the data to control systems. Different types of temperature transmitters include thermistors, which use changes in electrical resistance to measure temperature; bimetallic temperature transmitters, which rely on the bending of two different metals to detect temperature changes; resistive temperature detectors, which measure temperature using changes in electrical resistance; fiber optic temperature transmitters, which use light signals to measure temperature; and thermocouples, which generate a voltage proportional to temperature differences.</p></p>
