<p><p>Ticket Machine Market includes two main types: E-Ticket machines and E-Kiosks. E-Ticket machines are electronic ticketing systems that allow customers to purchase tickets online and receive a digital ticket via email or mobile app. They offer convenience and speed, making them popular in transportation and event industries. E-Kiosks, on the other hand, are self-service machines located in public areas where customers can purchase tickets on-site. They provide a quick and easy way to buy tickets without the need for human interaction. Both types cater to different customer preferences and needs in the ticketing industry.</p></p>
