Whole-home systems involve central air conditioning units that cool the entire house through a system of ductwork. Split systems, on the other hand, consist of an outdoor compressor unit and indoor air handling unit that work together to cool specific rooms or zones in the house. Both types of systems require regular maintenance to ensure they operate efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
