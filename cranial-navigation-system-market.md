<p><p>The Cranial Navigation System market includes two main types: Optical System and Electromagnetic System. Optical Systems utilize cameras to track instruments and navigate through a patient's anatomy. Electromagnetic Systems use sensors to create a real-time 3D map of the patient's anatomy and guide surgical tools. Both systems offer precise navigation capabilities for neurosurgery and other delicate procedures, improving accuracy and reducing risk for patients. The choice between the two types depends on factors such as cost, accuracy, and ease of use.</p></p>
