<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The TFT LCD Driver ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Source Driver</li><li>Gate Driver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TFT LCD driver IC market is primarily divided into two main types: source drivers and gate drivers. Source driver ICs manage pixel signal generation for each column of the display, directly driving the voltage levels that control light intensity and color. Gate driver ICs, on the other hand, control the timing and activation of rows in the display, ensuring that data is sequentially sent to the appropriate pixels. Both types are essential for the seamless operation of TFT LCD panels in various applications.</p></p>
