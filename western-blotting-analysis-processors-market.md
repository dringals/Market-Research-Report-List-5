<p><p>Western blotting analysis processors market can be categorized into three types based on the blotting systems used: dry blotting systems, semi-dry blotting systems, and others. Dry blotting systems involve the transfer of proteins from gel to membrane using a buffer system without the need for liquid transfer, while semi-dry blotting systems use a buffer system and electrical current for protein transfer. The "others" category includes variations and combinations of different blotting techniques used in western blot analysis.</p></p>
