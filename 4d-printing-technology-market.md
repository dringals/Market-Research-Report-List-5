<p><p>4D printing technology market includes various types such as Inkjet Printing, Melt Deposition Modeling (FDM), Direct Ink Writing (DIW), Stereolithography (SLA), Electron Beam Melting (EBM), and Selective Laser Melting (SLM). Inkjet Printing involves the deposition of materials in a layer-by-layer fashion. FDM uses thermoplastic filament to build objects. DIW utilizes a nozzle to extrude material onto a substrate. SLA uses a UV laser to solidify liquid resin. EBM and SLM both use a laser or electron beam to melt metal powder for 3D printing.</p></p>
