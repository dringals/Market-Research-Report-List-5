<p><p>The cemetery service market can be categorized into two main types: At-Need and Pre-Need. At-Need refers to services arranged and purchased at the time of a loved one's death, typically involving immediate burial or cremation arrangements. Pre-Need, on the other hand, involves planning and purchasing cemetery services in advance of need, allowing individuals to make their own arrangements and alleviate financial and emotional burdens on their families in the future. Both types cater to different stages of the funeral planning process.</p></p>
