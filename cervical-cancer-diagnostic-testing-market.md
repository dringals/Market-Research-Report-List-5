<p><p>Cervical cancer diagnostic testing market includes various types of tests such as Pap testing, HPV testing, colposcopy, and cervical biopsies. Pap testing involves collecting cells from the cervix to check for abnormal changes. HPV testing detects the presence of high-risk HPV strains that can cause cervical cancer. Colposcopy is a procedure that allows doctors to closely examine the cervix for abnormal areas. Cervical biopsies involve removing a small piece of tissue from the cervix for further examination to determine the presence of cancer cells.</p></p>
