<p><strong>The Multi-Touch Screen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Technology</li><li>Resistive Technology</li><li>Acoustic Technology</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multi-touch screen technology can be divided into different types based on how they function. Capacitive technology works by sensing the electrical properties of the human body to detect touch. Resistive technology uses pressure to detect touch, making it less sensitive than capacitive screens. Acoustic technology uses sound waves to detect touch, offering a high level of accuracy. Other types of multi-touch screen technology include infrared and optical sensors, which have their own unique capabilities and advantages in the market.</p></p>
