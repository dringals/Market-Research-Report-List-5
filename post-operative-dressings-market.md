<p><strong>The Post Operative Dressings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbent Dressings</li><li>Nonabsorbent Dressings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The post-operative dressings market is categorized into absorbent and nonabsorbent dressings. Absorbent dressings are designed to manage exudate from surgical wounds, promoting healing while preventing skin maceration. They often contain materials that wick moisture away from the wound. Nonabsorbent dressings, on the other hand, serve primarily as protective barriers, preventing contaminants and supporting a sterile environment without absorbing fluids. Both types play crucial roles in post-surgical care, enhancing patient recovery and minimizing infection risks.</p></p>
