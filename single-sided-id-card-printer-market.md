<p><p>Single-Sided ID Card Printer market includes Inkjet Printers and Dye Sub Printers. Inkjet printers use liquid ink sprayed onto the card surface, offering vibrant colors and precise detailing. Dye sublimation printers transfer dye onto the card using heat, producing high-quality, long-lasting images. Both printer types cater to different printing needs and budgets, with dye sub printers typically offering higher print quality and durability, while inkjet printers are more cost-effective for lower volume printing.</p></p>
