<p><p>The Metal Core Printed Circuit Board (MCPCB) market is categorized into three main types: Aluminum Core PCB, Copper Core PCB, and Alloys Core PCB. Aluminum Core PCBs are known for their excellent thermal conductivity and lightweight properties, making them suitable for various LED applications. Copper Core PCBs offer superior thermal and electrical conductivity, ideal for high-performance electronics. Alloys Core PCBs, made from composite materials, balance cost and performance, providing versatility in applications requiring both heat dissipation and mechanical strength.</p></p>
