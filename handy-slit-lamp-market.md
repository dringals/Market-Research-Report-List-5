<p><ul><li>Monocular Slit Lamp</li><li>Binocular Slit Lamp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Handy Slit Lamp market is divided into two main types: monocular and binocular slit lamps. Monocular slit lamps feature a single viewing tube, making them portable and easier to maneuver, ideal for general examinations. In contrast, binocular slit lamps have two viewing tubes, allowing for enhanced depth perception and detailed observations, often preferred in clinical settings. Both types cater to different user needs, with monocular versions focusing on convenience and binocular models emphasizing precision in diagnosis and treatment.</p></p>
