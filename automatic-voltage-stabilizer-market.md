<p><p>Automatic Voltage Stabilizers are categorized into two main types: Single Phase and 3 Phase. Single Phase Automatic Voltage Stabilizers are designed to regulate voltage fluctuations in single-phase electrical systems, commonly used in residential and small commercial settings. On the other hand, 3 Phase Automatic Voltage Stabilizers are used in larger industrial and commercial applications, where three-phase power supply is required. Both types of stabilizers work by automatically adjusting the voltage output to maintain a consistent level and protect equipment from damage due to fluctuations in power supply.</p></p>
