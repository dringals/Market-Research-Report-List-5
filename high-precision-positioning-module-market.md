<p><p>High precision positioning modules are categorized into centimeter-level and decimeter-level markets based on the accuracy of positioning they provide. Centimeter-level modules offer highly accurate positioning within a range of a few centimeters, making them suitable for applications requiring precise location tracking such as surveying and autonomous vehicles. On the other hand, decimeter-level modules provide slightly lower accuracy, typically within a range of a few decimeters, and are commonly used in applications where less precise positioning is acceptable, such as precision agriculture and construction.</p></p>
