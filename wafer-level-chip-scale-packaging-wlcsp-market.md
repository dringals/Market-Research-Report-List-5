<p><p>Wafer Level Chip Scale Packaging (WLCSP) market includes Redistribution and Molded Substrate types. Redistribution WLCSP involves adding an additional layer of metal redistribution lines on top of the wafer to connect the chip's pads with the external connections. Molded Substrate WLCSP incorporates a molded substrate with embedded components, offering enhanced mechanical and thermal properties. Both types of WLCSP packaging provide smaller form factors, improved performance, and cost-effective solutions for semiconductor devices.</p></p>
