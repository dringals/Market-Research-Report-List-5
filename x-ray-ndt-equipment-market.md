<p><ul><li>CR</li><li>DR</li><li>CT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>X-Ray NDT Equipment market comprises three main types: CR, DR, and CT. Computed Radiography (CR) uses a phosphor imaging plate to capture X-ray images for analysis. Digital Radiography (DR) uses digital sensors to capture X-ray images, eliminating the need for film processing. Computed Tomography (CT) combines X-ray technology with computer processing to create detailed 3D images of objects for inspection. Each of these technologies offers unique advantages in non-destructive testing applications, catering to diverse market needs and preferences.</p></p>
