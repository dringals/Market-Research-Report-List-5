<p><strong>The 3D Imaging TOF Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct ToF (dToF)</li><li>Indirect ToF (iToF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Imaging Time-of-Flight (ToF) sensor market is divided into two main types: Direct ToF (dToF) and Indirect ToF (iToF). dToF sensors measure the time light takes to travel directly to an object and back, enabling precise depth mapping and real-time applications. In contrast, iToF sensors utilize phase shift measurements to infer depth, ensuring high resolution and performance in varied lighting conditions. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, gaming, and augmented reality.</p></p>
