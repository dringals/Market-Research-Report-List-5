<p><p>Vascular Access Devices market includes various types such as Central Venous Catheters (CVC), Implantable Ports, Dialysis Catheters, and Peripherally Inserted Central Catheters (PICC). CVC catheters are used for high-risk patients requiring long-term access, while implantable ports provide long-term access for medications and fluids. Dialysis catheters are specifically designed for dialysis treatments, and PICC catheters are inserted in a peripheral vein and threaded through to a larger vein, often used for long-term antibiotic therapy or chemotherapy.</p></p>
