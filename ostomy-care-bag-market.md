<p><p>Ostomy care bags come in three main types: one-piece system, two-piece system, and skin barrier. The one-piece system consists of the bag and skin barrier as a single unit, making it easy to apply and remove. The two-piece system separates the bag and skin barrier, providing more flexibility in changing and emptying the bag. The skin barrier market includes products specifically designed to protect the skin around the stoma from irritation or damage. Each type offers unique advantages to meet the diverse needs of ostomy patients.</p></p>
