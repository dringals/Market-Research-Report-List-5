<p><ul><li>Static Surface Tension Measurement Equipment</li><li>Dynamic Surface Tension Measurement Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Static Surface Tension Measurement Equipment refers to instruments that measure surface tension of a liquid while it is at rest, such as Wilhelmy plates or Du Nouy rings. Dynamic Surface Tension Measurement Equipment, on the other hand, measures surface tension of a liquid in motion, such as drop volume tensiometers or bubble pressure tensiometers. Both types of instruments are essential for understanding the properties of liquids and are commonly used in research, quality control, and product development in various industries.</p></p>
