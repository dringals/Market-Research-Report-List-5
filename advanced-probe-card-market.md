<p><p>Advanced probe card market can be categorized into three main types: vertical, MEMS, and other markets. Vertical probe cards are specifically designed for testing vertical microelectronic devices such as DRAM, flash memory, and logic ICs. MEMS probe cards are used for testing MEMS devices like accelerometers, gyroscopes, and pressure sensors. Other markets typically refer to probe cards used for testing other types of electronic components and devices. Each type of probe card serves a specific purpose and offers different features to meet the testing requirements of various electronic applications.</p></p>
