<p><p>Glow Discharge Spectrometer (GDMS) is a powerful analytical tool used for elemental analysis in various industries. There are two main types of GDMS systems - dc-GDMS and rf-GDMS. dc-GDMS utilizes direct current to generate the glow discharge plasma, while rf-GDMS uses radio frequency power. Both systems offer high sensitivity and accuracy in detecting trace elements in solid samples. They are widely used in material science, environmental monitoring, and quality control applications.</p></p>
