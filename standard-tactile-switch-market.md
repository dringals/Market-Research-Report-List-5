<p><strong>The Standard Tactile Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface-Mounting</li><li>Throgh-hole Mounting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Standard tactile switches are commonly categorized into two main types based on their mounting methods: surface-mounting and through-hole mounting. </p><p>Surface-mounting tactile switches are soldered directly onto a printed circuit board, making them more compact and suitable for high-density applications. On the other hand, through-hole mounting tactile switches are inserted through holes in the PCB and soldered on the opposite side, providing greater mechanical stability and reliability. Both types cater to different design requirements and are widely used in various electronic devices.</p></p>
