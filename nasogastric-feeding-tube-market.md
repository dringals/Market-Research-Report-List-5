<p><p>The Nasogastric Feeding Tube market includes three main types: Levin Catheter, Salem Sump Catheter, and Dobhoff Tube. Levin Catheter is a single-lumen tube used for short-term feeding. Salem Sump Catheter is a double-lumen tube with one lumen for feeding and the other for suction or decompression. Dobhoff Tube is a small-bore feeding tube that is more flexible and designed for long-term use. Each type of tube has specific features and indications for different patient needs in the medical field of nutrition support.</p></p>
