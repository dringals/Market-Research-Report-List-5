<p><p>Inspection lamps are available in two main types: magnetic and non-magnetic. Magnetic inspection lamps have a strong magnet that allows them to easily attach to metal surfaces, providing hands-free illumination in various working environments. On the other hand, non-magnetic inspection lamps do not have a magnet and typically rely on clamps or stands for positioning. Both types offer portable and adjustable lighting solutions for automotive, industrial, and home inspection tasks. Customers can choose between magnetic and non-magnetic lamps based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
