<p><p>Photoelectric proximity switches are used in various industries for object detection. DC Type photoelectric proximity switches operate using a direct current power source, providing high accuracy and reliability. On the other hand, AC Type photoelectric proximity switches use an alternating current power source, offering longer sensing ranges and resistance to electromagnetic interference. Both types cater to different applications and preferences, with DC Type switches being favored for their stability and AC Type switches for their longer range and interference resistance.</p></p>
