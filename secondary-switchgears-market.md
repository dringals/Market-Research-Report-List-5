<p><strong>The Secondary Switchgears Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-insulated</li><li>Vacuum-insulated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Secondary switchgears are divided into gas-insulated and vacuum-insulated types in the market. Gas-insulated switchgears use sulfur hexafluoride gas to insulate and arc-quench the switchgear components, providing high reliability and performance in medium to high voltage applications. Vacuum-insulated switchgears, on the other hand, use vacuum as the insulating medium, offering similar benefits of reliability and performance but with lower environmental impact and maintenance requirements. Both types are widely used in electrical distribution systems for their compact design and efficient operation.</p></p>
