<p><p>Pulse-tube Cryocoolers come in two main market types: Single Stage and Two Stage. Single Stage cryocoolers have a simpler design and are typically used for applications requiring cooling temperatures above 10 Kelvin. On the other hand, Two Stage cryocoolers have a more complex design and can achieve lower temperatures below 10 Kelvin. These cryocoolers use a two-step cooling process, making them suitable for applications that require very low temperatures, such as in scientific research and medical imaging.</p></p>
