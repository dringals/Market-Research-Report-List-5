<p><p>Transmission Control Units (TCUs) are essential components in modern vehicles that optimize power delivery and fuel efficiency. There are two main types of TCUs - Variable Transmission Control Units and Continuously Variable Transmission Control Units. Variable TCUs are known for their ability to adjust gear ratios in discrete steps, providing a smoother driving experience. On the other hand, Continuously Variable TCUs offer an infinite number of gear ratios, allowing for seamless acceleration and improved fuel economy. Both types of TCUs play a crucial role in enhancing the overall performance of vehicles.</p></p>
