<p><strong>The Dental X ray Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intraoral X-Ray Imaging</li><li>Extraoral X-Ray Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental X-ray device market is divided into two primary types: intraoral and extraoral X-ray imaging. Intraoral X-ray imaging occurs within the mouth, capturing detailed images of teeth and surrounding structures, essential for diagnosing cavities, infections, and bone loss. Extraoral X-ray imaging, on the other hand, takes images from outside the mouth, providing broader views useful for assessing jaw structures and overall dental health. Together, these modalities enhance diagnostic accuracy and treatment planning in dentistry.</p></p>
