<p><p>Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) wafers are used in various applications such as solar cells, LEDs, and transistors. The two main types of GaAs wafers in the market are LEC (Liquid Encapsulated Czochralski) grown GaAs and VGF (Vertical Gradient Freeze) grown GaAs. LEC grown GaAs wafers have high purity and better crystal quality, making them suitable for high-frequency devices. On the other hand, VGF grown GaAs wafers are known for their low defect density and high electron mobility, making them ideal for high-power and high-speed devices.</p></p>
