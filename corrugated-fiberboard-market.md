<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Corrugated Fiberboard Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-flute</li><li>B-flute</li><li>C-flute</li><li>E-flute</li><li>F-flute</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corrugated fiberboard is classified into different types based on the size and shape of the ridges or corrugations within the material. A-flute is the largest and thickest type, providing excellent cushioning and stacking strength. B-flute is smaller, offering good crush resistance and printability. C-flute is the most common type, striking a balance between cushioning and stacking strength. E-flute is fine and provides a smooth surface for high-quality printing. F-flute is the smallest type, ideal for lightweight packaging requiring a high-quality print finish.</p></p>
