<p><p>Dry Scroll Vacuum Pumps are categorized into two main types: Single-sided Scroll and Double-sided Scroll. Single-sided Scroll pumps have one spiral rotating scroll and one stationary scroll, while Double-sided Scroll pumps have two spiral scrolls that rotate in opposite directions. Single-sided Scroll pumps are commonly used for lighter-duty applications, while Double-sided Scroll pumps are preferred for heavier-duty applications. Both types are known for their oil-free operation, low noise levels, and minimal maintenance requirements, making them popular choices in various industries.</p></p>
