<p><p>Surgical medical masks are available in two main market types: disposable masks and reusable masks. Disposable masks are intended for single-use and are typically made of non-woven polypropylene material. They are lightweight, easy to use, and designed for optimal protection during medical procedures. Reusable masks, on the other hand, are made of washable fabric materials like cotton or polyester. They can be reused multiple times after proper cleaning and disinfection. Both types of masks play a crucial role in preventing the spread of infections in healthcare settings and the community.</p></p>
