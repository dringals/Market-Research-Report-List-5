<p><p>Anti biofilm wound dressing is a type of dressing that is designed to prevent or disrupt the formation of biofilms on wounds, which are bacterial communities that can impair wound healing and increase the risk of infection. These dressings typically contain antimicrobial agents or other active ingredients that target and disrupt biofilms, helping to promote faster wound healing and reduce the risk of complications.</p><p>The Anti biofilm Wound Dressing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.60% during the forecast period.
