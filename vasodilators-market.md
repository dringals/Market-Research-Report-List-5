<p><strong>The Vasodilators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Arterial Dilators</li><li>Venous Dilators</li><li>Mixed Dilators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vasodilators market is classified into three main types: arterial dilators, venous dilators, and mixed dilators. Arterial dilators specifically target the arteries, reducing peripheral resistance and lowering blood pressure, making them ideal for conditions like hypertension. Venous dilators primarily relax the veins, decreasing venous return and reducing cardiac workload, useful in heart failure. Mixed dilators affect both arteries and veins, providing broader applications by balancing blood flow and pressure, thus addressing multiple cardiovascular issues comprehensively.</p></p>
