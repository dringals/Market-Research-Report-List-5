<p><ul><li>DLP</li><li>LCD</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multimedia projectors are categorized into three main types: DLP (Digital Light Processing), LCD (Liquid Crystal Display), and Others. DLP projectors use tiny mirrors to reflect light and produce images, offering high contrast ratios and sharp image quality. LCD projectors work by passing light through liquid crystal panels to create images, providing vibrant colors and sharp details. Other types of projectors may include LED, laser, or LCOS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) technologies, each offering unique features and benefits to cater to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
