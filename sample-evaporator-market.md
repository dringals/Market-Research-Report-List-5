<p><p>Sample evaporators are essential laboratory equipment that help in the separation of solvents, gases, solids, powders, and other substances from a sample. Solvent evaporators are used to remove solvents from liquid samples, while gas evaporators are used to separate gases from a mixture. Solid evaporators are employed to remove solids from a liquid sample, and powder evaporators are utilized to obtain dry powder samples. Other types of evaporators may cater to specific sample types not covered by the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
