<p><p>The consumer electronics electromechanical switch market consists of various types including Tactile, Rotary, Encoder, Toggle, Push, Detect, Micro, Dip, and Other types such as Power, Slide, and Joy Stick switches. Tactile switches provide a tactile feedback when pressed, while rotary switches are rotated to make contact. Encoder switches convert rotary motion into digital signals. Toggle switches have a lever for on/off control. Push switches are pressed to make contact. Detect switches sense changes in environment. Micro switches are small snap-action switches. Dip switches have multiple independent switches in one package. Other types like Power, Slide, and Joy Stick switches cater to specific functions in consumer electronics.</p></p>
