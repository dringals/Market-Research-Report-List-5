<p><p>Bactericides market types include active chlorine, iodine, concentrated alcohols, phenolic substances, and cationic surfactants. Active chlorine bactericides work by releasing chlorine to kill bacteria. Iodine acts as an antiseptic and disinfectant. Concentrated alcohols denature proteins in bacterial cell membranes. Phenolic substances disrupt bacterial cell walls and membranes. Cationic surfactants have a positive charge that disrupts bacterial cell walls. Each type of bactericide works in a different way to effectively eliminate bacteria and prevent their growth.</p></p>
