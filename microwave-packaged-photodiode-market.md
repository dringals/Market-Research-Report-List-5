<p><ul><li>AC Coupled</li><li>DC Coupled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AC coupled photodiodes in the microwave packaged photodiode market are designed to work with alternating current signals. They are ideal for applications where the signal needs to be amplified or processed by an AC-coupled circuit. On the other hand, DC coupled photodiodes are designed to work with direct current signals. They are suited for applications where the signal needs to be processed without any coupling capacitors. Both types of photodiodes have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
