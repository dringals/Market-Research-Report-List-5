<p><p>AC Cross Flow Fans are classified into two main types based on the shaft design: Single Shaft and Double Shaft. Single Shaft fans have a motor and impeller connected to a single shaft, providing a straightforward design and easier maintenance. On the other hand, Double Shaft fans have separate shafts for the motor and impeller, offering higher efficiency and better balance. Both types cater to different applications and requirements in the market, serving various industries such as HVAC, electronics, and automotive.</p></p>
