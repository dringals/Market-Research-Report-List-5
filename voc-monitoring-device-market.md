<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>VOC monitoring devices are used to detect and measure volatile organic compounds in the environment. Two common types of VOC monitoring devices are PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-oxide Semiconductor sensors. </p><p>PID devices work by ionizing gas molecules in a sample and measuring the resulting current, providing rapid and sensitive detection of a wide range of VOCs. </p><p>Metal-oxide Semiconductor sensors operate by sensing changes in electrical resistance when exposed to VOCs, providing continuous monitoring of air quality. Both types of devices are essential tools in ensuring workplace and environmental safety.</p></p>
